Explore The Beauty
of Medewi

Medewi Village is located in the Jembrana Regency, about 72 km from Denpasar. Known as one of Bali's hidden gems, Medewi offers pristine natural landscapes and a way of life steeped in tradition. With its clean beaches, perfect surfing waves, and serene atmosphere, Medewi has become a favorite spot for travelers looking to experience the untouched beauty of Bali.
